About Us

Café Aroma is a fresh new spot near Notre Dame College, Motijheel — brewed up by four young minds obsessed with good coffee and good vibes. We’re all about crafting a space that feels modern, relaxed, and inspiring — where you can sip, chill, and connect without breaking the bank.

For us, coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s a moment. A pause. A shared story. At Café Aroma, every cup comes with warmth, great taste, and a cozy corner waiting for students, teachers, and professionals to recharge or unwind.

Our crew stays grounded in quality and comfort, always chasing that perfect balance of flavor and feel. From global blends to local favorites, Café Aroma hopes to become Dhaka’s go-to name for coffee lovers who appreciate a little soul in every sip.
